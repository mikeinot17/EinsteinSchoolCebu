ESC Program

We offer a diverse array of academic programs catering to a range of interests and career aspirations, ensuring that every student can find a path that suits their individual goals and ambitions.

Learn More
Preschool

Preschool is early education for 3 to 5-year-olds, emphasizing play and basic skill-building to prepare them for kindergarten.

Grade school

Primary education includes grades 1 to 3, focusing on essential skills like reading, math, and basic sciences.

Junior HighSchool

Junior high school, grades 7 to 10, offers foundational and specialized education, bridging the transition from elementary to high school levels.

Senior HighSchool

Senior high school, grades 11 and 12, offers specialized tracks like STEM, ABM, HUMSS, ICT, and HE, preparing students for careers or further education.
